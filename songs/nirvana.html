Rape Me
Tuning: Half step down all strings [Eb, Ab, Db, Gb, Bb, eb]
Chord progression of the main riff uses the shapes of Amaj, Cmaj, Emaj, Gmaj in that order.
The strumming pattern is two down strokes on the A and E shapes, and the jangly bits on the C and G shapes.
Since the tuning is down a semitone, the resulting chords are Ab B Eb and Gb. The chord pattern in the key of B major is VII I V III assuming a key of B major, although the chord numberings is not concordant with the Ionian mode, which is viio I V iii.
