Chords Progressions

Major Scale for C major are the white keys on a piano.

C D E F G A B C
Root Whole Whole Half Whole Whole Whole Half
1 2 3 4 5 6 7 8

Build each chord as a triad. A triad is a group of three notes that are all a 3rd apart.

  • C: C-E-G
  • D: D-F-A
  • E: E-G-B
  • F: F-A-C
  • G: G-B-D
  • A: A-C-E
  • B: B-D-F

Now determine the number of semitones from the root.

  • C: 0-4-7
  • D: 0-3-7
  • E: 0-3-7
  • F: 0-4-7
  • G: 0-4-7
  • A: 0-3-7
  • B: 0-3-6

The number of semitones reveals the chord name.

  • C: C major
  • D: D minor
  • E: E minor
  • F: F major
  • G: G major
  • A: A minor
  • B: B diminished

This is commonly written as 

I ii iii IV V vi viib5
